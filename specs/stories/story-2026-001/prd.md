## Summary

This Story implements Phase 1 of the network circumvention guide reorganization project. The goal is to reorganize 13 existing markdown files in `docs/proxy/` into 9 clear topic chapters, update with 2026 technical information, enhance navigation, and improve overall documentation quality.
